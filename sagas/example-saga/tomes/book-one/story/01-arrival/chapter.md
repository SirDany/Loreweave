# Arrival at Vellmar

@character/bella slapped the snow off her cloak as the gates of @location/vellmar
came into view. "Skol, southerners!" she called, grinning wide. The word @term/skol
carried on the wind.

Beside her, @character/aaron said nothing. He rarely did.

"Grukh night ahead," she muttered, glancing at the sky. @term/grukh was her
favourite complaint; she used it like punctuation.
