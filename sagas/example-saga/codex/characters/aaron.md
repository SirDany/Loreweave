## Concept
- A northern soldier who deliberately avoids the Northern Slang — he grew up
  mocked for his stutter and trained himself to speak "plain".
- Carries guilt from Vellmar's fall; his arc in Book Two is reconciliation.

## Voice
Terse. Short declarative sentences. Never uses northern slang.
